Research

Title: Digital Marketing and Psychological Aspects of Consumer Buying Behavior

Abstract

This research examines the effects of digital marketing on consumer’s psychological buying behavior by studying various academic and non-academic literature. Digital marketing affects the learning aspect of the consumer’s psychological behavior. Furthermore, digital marketing has an impact on the motivational and perceptual aspects of consumers' psychological behavior. However, firms are not entirely aware of the effects of digital marketing on the psychological behavior of clients. The findings of the study are significant on the grounds that this will assist with understanding the impact of digital marketing and investigate the mental conduct of clients, for example, learning, motivational, and perceptual aspects, while doing any type of business. It was concluded that consumer psychological buying behavior is heavily influenced by digital marketing. This research is important in the field of business since this research focuses on scholars of the business field and the business firms that are trying out digitalization to grow their business.
